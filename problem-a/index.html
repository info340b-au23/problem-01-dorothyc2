Mulan

Mulan is a daughter that learns that her aged father is being asked to go into war. In an effort to protect him, Mulan decides to take his place and disguise herself as a male soldier. She can be described as courageous and resilient. Despite the trials she faces while preparing to fight, Mulan remains steadfast and loyal in her commitment to serve.
